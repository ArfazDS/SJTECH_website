EIIIS: Revolutionizing Vigilance and AT&C Loss Reduction

Discover how SJTech Solutions's flagship SaaS utilizes Machine Learning and Digital Twin technology to predict energy theft...

Electricity Distribution Utilities (DISCOMs) bleed millions annually due to Aggregate Technical and Commercial (AT&C) losses. Traditional checking is manual, prone to error, and inefficient. Enter EIIIS.

The Power of AI in Vigilance

The Enterprise Intelligent Information Infrastructure System (EIIIS) is our proprietary AI/ML-driven SaaS. Instead of blind sweeps, EIIIS ingests massive amounts of consumer billing and metering data. Using complex Machine Learning algorithms, it predicts exactly where and how energy theft or meter tampering is occurring.

Digital Twin Technology

EIIIS doesn't just read data; it maps the physical grid. By utilizing Digital Twin technology, we simulate localized grid networks to identify technical anomalies versus commercial theft. This allows DISCOMs to deploy their vigilance teams with surgical precision.

Proven at Scale: MPMKVVCL & KESCO

The system's efficacy isn't theoretical. Over the last two years, EIIIS has been actively deployed at major distribution hubs including MPMKVVCL (Madhya Pradesh) and is currently executing in KESCO (Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh), resulting in massive arrear recovery and loss minimization.

The SJTech Advantage: Zero new hardware is required. EIIIS is completely hardware agnostic, integrating with traditional meters, digital meters, and modern AMR/smart-meter infrastructures seamlessly.

EIIP: AI-Driven Corporate Energy Optimization

How modern corporations are meeting their ESG targets and slashing utility bills by 20% by deploying SJTech Solutions's automated energy auditing logic.

As organizations face mounting pressure to meet ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) targets, tracking and reducing corporate energy footprints has become a board-level priority.

Automating the Energy Audit

Historically, energy audits are static snapshots taken once a year. The Enterprise Intelligent IoT Platform (EIIP) transforms this into a continuous, real-time process. Our AI integrates with a corporation's existing data streams to automatically identify parasitic power drains and inefficient usage patterns across vast facilities.

Guaranteed Bill Reduction

Because EIIP operates on an intelligent logic layer, it provides actionable, automated strategies. Organizations utilizing EIIP see considerable reductions in their electric charges and utility bills, often surpassing 20%, simply by eliminating waste invisible to the human eye.

Furthermore, EIIP ensures that enterprises maintain compliance with tightening global carbon tracking standards, securing their ESG ratings for future investments.
